We propose IE-CL, a novel contrastive learning framework that explicitly maximizes incremental information entropy. Our method introduces an entropy-expanding transformation and an entropy-preserving encoder, leading to more diverse and discriminative representations. Extensive experiments demonstrate consistent improvements over existing contrastive learning methods, particularly under small-batch regimes.
